ABOUT

Introducing the Cassette Mixer Magnitron-1, developed by Andrey Kurokhtin, also known as Shine Grooves. The Magnitron-1 is a one-of-a-kind device that allows you to play and mix two cassette decks, each with independent control over pitch, volume, and a three-band equalizer.

Magnitron-1 is not just a mixer; it's a complete musical instrument, opening up new creative possibilities for working with cassette tapes.

Andrey handcrafts each unit in Yekaterinburg as part of a limited series.

SPECIFICATIONS

Tape Mechanism
Speed Nominal 4.76 cm/s (adjustable ±50%)
Wow and Flutter 0.7 – 1%
Frequency Response Type I: 40 Hz – 14 kHz / Type II: 40 Hz – 17 kHz / Type IV: 30 Hz – 21 kHz
Stereo Tracks 2 (Stereo)
Mixer Panel
Channels 2, with independent pitch control per deck
Equalizer 3-band EQ per channel (Bass, Mid, Treble with boost/cut)
Pre-listening Available in headphones with smooth switching
Master Volume Rotary knob
Inputs and Outputs
Line Inputs 2 (Stereo RCA)
Phono Inputs 2 (Stereo RCA)
Master Output 1 (Stereo RCA)
Recording Output 1 (Stereo RCA)
Headphone Output 1 (6.3 mm Jack)
Indicators
Type Stereo VU meters with calibration capability
Function Displays summed stereo signal (Deck 1 + Deck 2)
Power and Dimensions
Power Supply 200-230V AC, 50/60 Hz
Dimensions 492 mm × 386 mm × 123 mm
Weight 6 kg
Operating Environment
Temperature Range -5°C to +45°C
Humidity Up to 80%
